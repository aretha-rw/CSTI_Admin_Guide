The Front-End Website

The front-end of a site refers to the pages which are visible to the public. The CSTI front-end includes access to class registration, user accounts, contact information, and general background information on CSTI.



The Top Menu

You can navigate to each section of the site using the menu at the top of each page.

Learning Experiences (1) is where learners can register for open classes.

My Account (2) leads to the login portal.

Contact (3) helps learners get in touch with support.

About, Trauma-Responsive Care, Guidelines, and FAQs provide useful background information on CSTI and its sponsors.

Learning Experiences

When you first enter the registration page, you’ll see a list of all currently available classes.

Clicking Details (1) will open a pop-up window with more information on the class, including its capacity, facilitator, and street address if applicable.

Web-based trainings may be added to your cart immediately by clicking Add to Cart (2). In-person classes require learners to select first a region (3) then a start date (4) before they may add the class to their cart.



When you click Add to Cart, you’ll see a status icon (1) indicating the class’s availability and, if the class is open, an Added (2) icon below it with the option to remove the class from your cart (3). Once you’ve added every class you need to your cart, click Register (4) at the bottom of the page. A message will appear informing you of your status in each class from your cart.

Remember, adding classes to your cart alone will not register you for the class. You must also click Register.

My Account



Clicking My Account while not logged in will take you to a portal where you may log in (1), create a new account (2), or reset your password (3).

If you are logged in, My Account will direct you to your Profile.
