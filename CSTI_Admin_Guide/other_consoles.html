Other Consoles

All other consoles contain more limited versions of Admin Console functions, but as you may need to assist a learner in their use, it’s helpful to be familiar with what functions appear in each console.

Training Organization Console

The Training Organization Console provides access to the class list functions of Manage Classes and Manage Hybrids, to all student profiles, and to all facilitators listed under that training organization.

It does not provide access to any course or class editing functions and users’ passwords will not be visible on their profiles.

Coaches Console

The Coaches Console allows coaches to view the profiles and training hours of all learners who have listed them as their coach. Users’ passwords will not be visible on their profiles.

Facilitators Console

The Facilitators Console gives facilitators access to the Grade Students functions of every class and hybrid for which they’ve been listed as a facilitator.

Reporting Console

The Reporting Console provides access to the same functions as the Manage Reports segment of the Admin Console.
